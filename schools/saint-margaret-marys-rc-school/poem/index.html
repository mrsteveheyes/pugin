Poem

The first day I met Pugin...

The first day I met Pugin I did not know what to think really. I thought his wife was quite poor, even though she was pretty her clothes looked rather scruffy. I think she was a bit common. At first she called me Mrs Shrewsbury! I mean goodness, I am LADY Shrewsbury, the wife of LORD Shrewsbury!

When Anne died I felt sorry for Pugin so I decided to mach him up with a new wife, but he refused! I dont know why, his child needs a mother and he needs a wife. He replied, "I can find my own wife. I'm thankful  for your effort though."  

Rihanna
St. Margaret Mary Primary Catholic School 2012
